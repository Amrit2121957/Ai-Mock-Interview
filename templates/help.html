Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my first mock interview?

For new users:

  1. Create an account by clicking "Sign Up" in the navigation
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Go to the "Interview" section
  4. Select your target job role from the dropdown
  5. Upload your resume in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format
  6. Click "Generate Interview Questions" to start
What file formats are supported for resume upload?

TalentMate supports the following resume formats:

  • PDF - Recommended format
  • DOCX - Microsoft Word documents
  • TXT - Plain text files

Maximum file size: 16MB

How is my interview performance scored?

Our AI scoring system evaluates your answers based on:

  • Content Quality - Relevance and completeness of your answer
  • Technical Knowledge - Use of appropriate technical terminology
  • Structure - Organization and clarity of your response
  • Detail Level - Depth and specificity of examples
  • Communication - Overall presentation and confidence

Scores range from 0-100, with detailed feedback for improvement.

Can I retake interviews or practice multiple times?

Yes! You can:

  • Take unlimited mock interviews
  • Practice with different job roles
  • Upload updated resumes anytime
  • Review all your past interview sessions in the "Review" section
  • Track your improvement over time
What job roles are supported?

TalentMate supports various technology roles including:

  • Full Stack Developer (Java, Python, .NET)
  • Salesforce Admin & Developer
  • Program/Business Analyst
  • Software Engineer
  • DevOps Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • Product Manager
  • UI/UX Designer

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions:
Resume Upload Failed

1Check file format (PDF, DOCX, TXT only)

2Ensure file size is under 16MB

3Try a different browser or clear cache

4Check your internet connection

Questions Not Generating

1Ensure you're logged in to your account

2Select a job role from the dropdown

3Make sure your resume uploaded successfully

4Wait for processing (may take 30-60 seconds)

Can't Access Interview or Review Pages

1Make sure you're logged in

2Clear browser cookies and cache

3Try logging out and logging back in

4Use a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
